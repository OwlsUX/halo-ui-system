FAQ

FAQ

The live reviews route closes with practical buying and setup questions for yards, travel, breeds, indoor beacons, and fence limits.

I don’t have a yard of my own, or I live in an apartment, rental home, condo or townhome. Can I still use the Halo System?

Of course! One amazing part of Halo is the portability and freedom it provides. You can set up Halo Fences instantly in any location: at a rental property, around your apartment building, at a family member or friend's house, the park, the beach—the opportunities are endless! In addition, Halo Beacons can be used anywhere inside to provide indoor support, and your Cesar Millan training will teach you how to be the best pet parent possible.

Will Halo work for my dog’s breed?

Yes! Today over 350 breeds are currently using Halo Collar. Halo is a proven system that works on any breed and any age, as long as they are over 5 months old and over 20 lbs. It doesn’t matter if your dog’s breed is stubborn, energetic, playful, or calm, or if they chase squirrels, like to swim or frequently visit your neighbors. Once you train your dog on Halo, it will work on any breed.

Will Halo work when I am out of cellular or Wi-Fi coverage (i.e. while camping or hiking in a remote location)?

If you intend to go to a zero-coverage area, you can set up your Halo Fences before you leave, and it will work seamlessly. Halo Fences are stored in the collar's memory and won't be lost even if/when it loses power or an internet connection.

Does Halo work indoors?

Yes! Indoors, the Halo Collar can prevent dogs from entering unwanted or unsafe areas. To do so, set up Bluetooth-based Keep Away Beacons that trigger fence-like alerts when your dog comes near them.

Is there a limit to the number, location, or size of Halo Fences I can create?

You can create an unlimited number of fences in your Halo app depending on your plan, and they can be created anywhere in the world. If you intend to go to a zero-coverage area, you can set up your Halo Fences before you leave, and it will work seamlessly. You can edit any fence, at any time (just be sure to practice this new boundary with your dog!). Each fence needs to include a minimum of 30ft x 30ft for your dog to roam safely, and can accommodate a maximum of over one thousand square miles.
