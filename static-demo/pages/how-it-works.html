FAQ

This is where Halo truly differentiates from other collars. Our patented, dog psychology-based method is intuitive and effective, which makes Halo far safer than other collars.

How does Halo keep my dog safely contained inside my Halo Fence?

When approaching a boundary, your dog automatically receives customized feedback that your dog will associate with a simple Keep Away command.

If your dog does not stop or turn around, escalating feedback is given until your dog responds. Once they stop or turn around, a Come Back command guides them back to safety.

What are Halo Fences and how do I create them?

Search an address or select any location on the map in the Halo App. A fence will be auto-generated based on property lines and Halo Fence guidelines in one tap. Easily edit your fences at any time. The GPS coordinates of the Halo Fence are automatically downloaded to the collar and remain there permanently (unless you edit or delete them). Halo Fences can’t be cut, damaged, jumped over, dug under, and don’t require any maintenance.

What types of feedback does the collar provide for my dog?

The Halo Collar is capable of providing six types of feedback:

  • Prevention feedback: Warning, Boundary, and Emergency
  • Encouragement feedback: Whistle, Good Dog, and Go Home

Each type of feedback has predefined default settings, but you can customize your dog’s settings at any time. Your dog’s feedback types can be set to use sounds, vibrations, or static.

Does Halo use static feedback? If so, when? Does it hurt?

Halo uses Static feedback that creates a sense of “touch” meant to mimic a tap on the shoulder (as opposed to shock collars which use an electronic pulse that is painful to your dog). This timely touch breaks your dog’s fixation in dangerous situations and distraction-filled environments to keep them out of harm’s way. You’re never required to use static with your dog’s Halo Collar, the Halo training process will help you customize the feedback that your dog will respond to.

Can my dog escape a Halo Fence?

Unlike all other GPS fence collars, Halo Fences are ‘Escape Proof’. Halo’s patented, intuitive and gentle feedback keeps escalating until your dog responds. It does not give up. Once your dog responds to the Keep Away command that you have trained it for, a Come Back command brings it back home. For your peace of mind, you are immediately notified once a fence breach is detected. Plus, live in-app tracking never stops providing protection.
